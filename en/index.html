About Me

Hello! I’m Lillie Thomasset.

I am a Creative Technologist based at the Atlas Institute (CU Boulder), where I balance my time as a student and a Lab Assistant. Outside the lab, I serve as an Administrative Assistant at the Parlando School of Musical Arts.

I'm interested in how my minors in Music and Japanese can inform my design work—using the structure of composition and the nuances of language to make digital experiences feel more human.
